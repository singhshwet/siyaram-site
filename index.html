Since 1978

From 1978 till today, through our fabrics and brands, we have carved a niche in the hearts and minds of generations of men.


The urge to innovate, the quest for artisanal fabrics from the Top Fabric Manufacturers in India, and the transmission of exceptional know-how have enabled the creation of world-class fashion fabrics that stand the test of time and forge the uniqueness of Siyaram’s.


We deeply understand men’s clothing needs and are on the cutting edge of innovation and technology to make their dreams a reality. Our legacy is the foundation on which our future is being built.
